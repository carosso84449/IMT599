Frequently Asked Questions

This page contains some of the most common questions regarding the ElectionGuard software and how elections work when ElectionGuard is implemented.

Can I check who I voted for in my officially casted ballot?

No. The site will only display whether your vote was correctly tallied in the election. For privacy purposes, actual votes have no way of being displayed after they have been cast.

Can others see who I voted for in my officially casted ballot?

No. No one, including the person who submitted their cast ballot can see the contents after it has been cast.

Is my name attached to the verification code feature?

No. Verification codes are anonymous and encrypted. Names are never displayed on confirmation codes.

What does my ballot status mean?

Cast - This status means that your ballot was received and is part of the election record

Spoiled - This status indicates that this ballot was not cast, the ballot was spoiled, and the contents of the ballot are available to be seen

Can someone find out who I am from a spoiled ballot?

Absolutely Not. All ballots whether they are cast or spoiled do not contain any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and are fully encrypted.
